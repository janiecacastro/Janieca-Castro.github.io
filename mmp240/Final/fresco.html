New Age Creations

Images I created about for an awareness campaign on Advanced Technologies and the Loss of Human Connection. The idea behind the first image is to show a fetus being “hatched” artificially, instead of a natural birth, while other "unhatched" or unborn fetus's are in the background, awaiting their arrival. This is working towards the idea of how the possibilities of advanced technologies in the future and how far will society take these advancement.
